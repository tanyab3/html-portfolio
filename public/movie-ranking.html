The best movies according to Tania

My top 3 movies of all time

Avatar

A science fiction film with spectacular visual effects. The main message of the film is a cautionary tale about the dangers of exploiting
and destroying the natural world and the importance of respecting and living in harmony with nature.

The life of Pi

A heartfelt and intense story of survival and triumph against the odds, with themes of faith, friendship, and perseverance.

Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter… and Spring

A beautiful Korean movie that reflects on our life’s journey, the choices we make, and subsequent consequences.
